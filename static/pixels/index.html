Add Website Link

Follow the below instructions to add a website link to your creations:

    Join Discord
  1. Join the Astral Credits Discord.
  2. Visit the ✅-register channel.
  3. Use the /register command and add your XAC address (Note: Skip this step if you have already registered to claim from the faucet).

Pixels added to the canvas by the registered address will now show the Website URL in the Info tab!

Pixel Planet - Powered by Astral Credits

Welcome to Pixel Planet! A DApp (Decentralized Application) Pixel Canvas powered by Astral Credits. Buy pixels using XAC, create amazing works and share them with the SONGBIRD community! All tile information is stored and modified by interacting with the Pixel Planet tile smart contract.

Instructions:

  1. Click the "Connect" button to connect your wallet to the Pixel Planet application.
  2. Select any tile on the canvas and enter in how many tokens you wish to pay for it (min price: 1 $XAC)
    (Note: Ensure the amount is higher than the current price paid for that tile.
  3. Click "Approve Spending" to allow the contract to spend a limited amount of tokens (This permission can be easily modified or revoked).
  4. Once confirmed, click "Buy Pixel" and approve your transaction.
  5. Once the transaction is confirmed, the tile will change to the color selected and will also display additional address and hyperlink details.
