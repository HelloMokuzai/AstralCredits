Make sure you have both the Flare and Songbird networks added.

Step 1: Burn sgbXAC

Burn however much sgbXAC you want to bridge by sending it to , receiving a 10:1 ratio of flareXAC in return.

If you weren't able to submit the TX hash the first time for whatever reason, you can manually enter it and try again.

Step 2: Submit TX Hash

Submit the TX hash of the burn, get a signature. Submit the signature to the Flare bridge contract, and get your flareXAC!
