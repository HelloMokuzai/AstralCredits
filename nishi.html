Nishi Coin

The Jewel of the Astral Credits Community

A Celestial Rarity, Unlocking Galactic Possibilities!

Named after one of the Astral Credits founding members, NISHI Coin started its life as a lighthearted meme-coin to promote our Discord tipping service. Thanks to its limited supply however - NISHI has evolved into a valuable utility token within the Astral Credits ecosystem!

Earn NISHI Coin by being active within the Astral Credits Community Discord and use it to unlock rare and exclusive perks!

  • Limited Supply

    NISHI Coin's are ultra-rare, making each coin highly coveted. With a limited supply of just 100,000 tokens.

  • Growing Utility

    Originally launched as a meme, NISHI Coin is being rebranded with real utility, allowing holders to purchase Tipbot awards and other future features within the community.

  • Free to Earn

    NISHI Coins are distributed to active members within the Astral Credits Community Discord. Join in the fun and be rewarded.

  • Supported by Mr.Tipbot

    Send NISHI to your friends on Discord instanly via Mr.Tipbot all on-chain and low cost.
